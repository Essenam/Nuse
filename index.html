About Nuse

We provide voice-activated financial services in local languages — offering access to microloans, financial education, business insights, savings, and insurance. We focus on rural women in the informal economy across Togo and beyond.

Our Solution

A single, simple experience that meets entrepreneurs where they are — on WhatsApp and voice — and grows with them.

Voice in local languages

Hands-free actions in Ewe & French: log sales/expenses, ask for advice, or request a microloan.

Microloans & savings

Smart eligibility, fair rates, reminders, and savings nudges tailored to each business.

Business insights

Weekly WhatsApp summaries and simple dashboards to track growth, costs, and profit.

Impact we measure

+ Savings created

Number of women who build and keep emergency savings for 3+ months.

+ Business stability

Improved inventory turnover and fewer stockouts through better planning.

+ Income & agency

Increased monthly income and decision-making power at home and in the community.
