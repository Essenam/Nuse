About Nuse

Giving a voice to women invisible to the financial system

Nuse is a voice-first, AI-enabled platform that makes financial services accessible, dignified, and data-driven for women in the informal economy.

  • Local languages (Ewe/French) and low-literacy UX
  • Financial education via short audio lessons
  • Business insights & nudges that build habits
  • Data-informed credit with transparent terms
